Anatomy of an Attack

Understanding the adversary.

Delivery Mechanism

Attackers use highly targeted spear-phishing emails, SMS (Smishing), and compromised social accounts to bypass spam filters. They employ urgency and authority to manipulate human psychology.

Evasive Infrastructure

Modern campaigns use compromised legitimate WordPress sites, fast-flux DNS, and reverse proxies (like Evilginx) to bypass 2FA and avoid detection by automated crawlers.

Visual Deception

Using stolen assets and identical CSS frameworks, attackers create pixel-perfect clones of Microsoft, Google, or banking portals, making visual identification nearly impossible for the average user.

Data Exfiltration

Once credentials or session tokens are captured, they are instantly transmitted via encrypted Telegram bots or automated APIs to dark web marketplaces for monetization.
